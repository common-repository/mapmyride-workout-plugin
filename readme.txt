== Description ==

This plugin will load your most recent workouts from MapMyRide/MapMyRun/MapMyHike/MapMyFitness and display them on your wordpress blog.

== Installation ==

1. Upload files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Enter the URL of your RSS feed from MapMyFitness

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I Need a MapMyRide Account? =

Yes you do.  We use your RSS feed from MapMyRide to show the latest workouts.
             
= How Do I Add The Widget to My Page =

1. Go to your admin page.
1. Go to the widget page.
1. Find the Map My Ride Widget and click Add
1. Click on Edit and add your RSS URL
        
= How Do I Create a Page With Content =

1. Go to your admin page
1. Click on the MapMyRide menu options
1. Add your configuration information
1. Create a new post or page
1. Put [mapmyride] in the content
